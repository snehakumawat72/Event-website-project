Gear up for the warmth of bonfires and the spirit of togetherness at our Lohri Lights College Fest! Our campus is set to glow with the radiant energy of Lohri, where traditions meet modern celebrations. Join us for lively folk performances, the crackle of bonfires, and the joyous beats of dhol. Immerse yourself in the festive fervor with traditional dances, lip-smacking Punjabi delights, and the heartwarming company of friends. Let's gather around the bonfire, share stories, and revel in the spirit of harvest and community. Lohri Lights is not just a fest; it's a celebration of abundance, joy, and the rich cultural tapestry that defines us. Come, light up your Lohri with us!
