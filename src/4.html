Prepare for a burst of colors and pure, unbridled joy at our Holi Hues College Fest! Our campus is about to become a kaleidoscope of laughter, dance, and vibrant hues as we celebrate the festival of Holi in all its glory. Dive into the rhythm of lively beats, witness mesmerizing cultural performances, and unleash your creativity with our Holi-inspired art installations. Join us for a day of uninhibited fun, where every splash of color paints a memory and every moment is a celebration of the vibrant tapestry of Holi!
