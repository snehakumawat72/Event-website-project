Experience the enchantment of our Ganesh Chaturthi College Fest, a spirited celebration that brings the joy of this revered Hindu festival to our campus. The fest is a colorful tapestry of cultural performances, traditional art, and delectable treats that embody the essence of Ganesh Chaturthi. From the rhythmic beats of dhol-tasha competitions to the eco-friendly idol-making workshops, the event bridges tradition and innovation, creating a festive atmosphere that resonates with both students and guests. The fest is not just a celebration; it's a heartfelt tribute to Lord Ganesha, fostering unity, creativity, and the rich cultural heritage that defines our college community.
